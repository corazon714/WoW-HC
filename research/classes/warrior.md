# Warrior — Hardcore Rehberi (Tüm Spec'ler)

**Versiyon:** Classic Era 1.15.x / Anniversary HC (Classic Fresh)
**Son Güncelleme:** 2026-06-18
**Class Difficulty (HC):** **YÜKSEK** (1-30), **ORTA** (30-60) — Wowhead HC Tier List: **C Tier** (en alt). HC'nin en zor leveling class'ı. Buna rağmen endgame raid'de "DPS + Tank" omurgası; survival yönü kaldıkça kazanılan class.
**Birincil Kaynak:** Wowhead Warrior HC Leveling Guide — https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/classes/warrior/hardcore-leveling-tips

## Özet
Warrior HC'de **erken seviye en zor**, **endgame en güçlü** class. Self-heal yok (bandage hariç), kaçış aracı zayıf (Intercept lvl 30+). Rage mekaniği her saldırıya bağımlı — boş rage = boş cooldown = ölüm. Ana strateji: **defansif overgear**, **dungeon priority** (Sul'thraze, Headstriker), **2H berserker** yerine 1H+shield güvenli.

## 1. Spec Seçimi (HC)

### 1.1 Arms (PvE solo leveling) — **Önerilen 1-40**
- **+10 (15) noktaya kadar Arms zorunlu**: Improved Heroic Strike, Deflection, Improved Rend, Tactical Mastery.
- Two-Handed Weapon Spec (+5% damage) → leveling spike.
- Mortal Strike (lvl 40 talent) — single-target burst.

### 1.2 Fury (PvE leveling alternatif) — **40-60 alternatif**
- Dual-wield faster killing. Cruelty, Unbridled Wrath, Enrage.
- HC'de risk: berserker stance + dual-wield = high incoming dmg.
- **Önerilmez** new HC oyuncu için.

### 1.3 Protection (Tank, group only) — **Endgame dungeon**
- Solo tehlikeli (düşük damage = uzun fight). Sadece grup içinde useful.
- Shield Slam (lvl 40), Last Stand, Shield Wall, Defiance (threat).

### 1.4 Önerilen HC Spec Build

#### Leveling 10-40 (Arms-heavy)
- **Tier 1**: 5/5 Improved Heroic Strike, 3/3 Deflection.
- **Tier 2**: 2/3 Improved Rend, 3/3 Tactical Mastery, 2/5 Iron Will.
- **Tier 3**: 5/5 Two-Handed Weapon Spec.
- **Tier 4**: 3/3 Impale, 1/1 Death Wish (Fury, alternative).

#### Lvl 40+ (Mortal Strike)
- Arms 31 noktaya tamamlayıp 1/1 Mortal Strike.
- Kalan 9-19 noktayı Fury'e (Cruelty, Unbridled Wrath) veya Prot'a (Toughness, Defiance) yatır.

### 1.5 Önerilen Endgame Build (Tank-leaning)
- **Fury/Prot 17/0/34** veya **Arms/Prot 20/0/31** — solo + grup hybrid.

## 2. HC-Özel Strateji

### 2.1 Defensive Stance > Berserker
- HC'de **Defensive Stance** ana stance olmalı (10% damage reduction, 5% threat).
- Berserker sadece **Berserker Rage** (fear/sap immunity) için CD'de aç-kapa.

### 2.2 Shield + 1H her zaman secondary slot
- Solo 2H ile fight başla → tehlike anında shield equip → Shield Block + Defensive Stance.
- "Equip mid-fight" macro:
  ```
  /equip Shield Name
  /equip 1H Weapon Name
  /cast Defensive Stance
  ```

### 2.3 Bandage Discipline
- Combat'tan çıktıktan sonra **HER ZAMAN** bandage.
- First Aid 300 priority (Tome of First Aid: Master quest).
- Mage food bağımlı değilsin = bandage altın.

### 2.4 Rage Management
- Open fight: Charge (Battle Stance) → 15 rage → switch Defensive → Sunder Armor x5 → Heroic Strike.
- "Bloodrage" (free 10 rage) her CD'de bas.
- Rage starvation = ölüm. Slow weapon (3.5+ speed) = az rage. **Fast weapon (2.4-2.7) tercih edilmeli leveling'de**.

### 2.5 Escape Tools
- **Charge** (Battle Stance, lvl 4): 25 yard initiator + stun.
- **Intercept** (Berserker Stance, lvl 30): 30 yard charge + stun.
- **Intimidating Shout** (lvl 22): 5 yard fear; **fear-into-pack** riski yüksek, dikkatli kullan.
- **Hamstring** (lvl 8): slow; kaçış için kit.
- **Disarm** (lvl 36): mob disable; humanoid only.
- **Berserker Rage** (lvl 32): fear/sap immunity 10 sn.
- **Last Stand** (Prot lvl 40): +30% max HP 20 sn.
- **Shield Wall** (Prot lvl 30): -75% damage 10 sn, 30 min CD.
- **Recklessness** (Berserker, lvl 30): +100% crit 15 sn ama -1 stam.

> **HC** Recklessness HC'de **kullanma** — incoming damage ölümcül.

## 3. Stat Öncelikleri

### Leveling 1-40
1. **Strength** (1 str = 2 AP)
2. **Stamina** (HC priority!)
3. **Agility** (crit, dodge, armor)

### Leveling 40-60
1. **Strength** > **Stamina** > **Agility** > **Crit %** > **Hit %**

### Endgame (dungeon tank)
1. **Stamina** > **Defense** (cap 315 vs lvl 60 boss) > **Strength** > **Agility**

---

## 4. Rotasyon

### Solo Leveling Rotation
```
Charge → Battle Shout → Sunder Armor (rage permitting) → Heroic Strike (next swing) →
Hamstring (if escape needed) → Bandage (post-combat)
```

### Mortal Strike Rotation (40+)
```
Charge → MS (cooldown) → Sunder x3 → HS (rage dump) → MS (CD) → finishing
```

### Tank Rotation (Dungeon)
```
Stance Defensive → Pull (ranged: Throw / Bow) → Sunder x5 → HS (rage maintenance) →
Demoralizing Shout → Revenge (every CD) → Taunt (add management)
```

### Mortal Strike Tank Hybrid (rare)
- Solo questing + tank kapasiteli — kompromise build.

## 8. PvP / Mak'gora

### Strengths
- **Charge + Intercept** combo: 2 stun in 6 sec.
- **Berserker Rage** + **Death Wish** burst.
- **Mortal Strike** healing reduction 50% (anti-healer).

### Weaknesses
- **Kited** by hunter/mage.
- **Feared into pack** by warlock.
- **Polymorphed** by mage.

### Mak'gora Tactics
- Open with **Charge** (Battle stance) → **Hamstring** → switch **Berserker** → **Recklessness** + **Death Wish** → **MS** burst.
- Free Action Potion before duel (Fear immune).
- Engineering grenade interrupt opponent cast.

## 9. HC-Spesifik Uyarılar

> **HC** Warrior'ın ölüm sebepleri (community data):
> 1. Multiple-add pull (#1, ~%40).
> 2. Fear-into-pack (Warlock/mob fear).
> 3. Berserker Stance içinde fight (incoming dmg +10%).
> 4. Rage starvation mid-fight (slow weapon).
> 5. No bandage post-combat (HP'ye girer).

### Survival Priority
1. **Asla 2+ mob aggro alma.** Charge tek targete.
2. **Defensive Stance** her zaman ana stance.
3. **Shield equipped** ölümcül fight'tan önce.
4. **Health potion** her zaman 5+ slotta.
5. **Bandage every combat exit.**
