# Druid — Hardcore Rehberi (Tüm Spec'ler)

**Versiyon:** Classic Era 1.15.x / Anniversary HC (Classic Fresh)
**Son Güncelleme:** 2026-06-18
**Class Difficulty (HC):** **DÜŞÜK-ORTA** — Wowhead HC Tier List: **A Tier**.
**Birincil Kaynak:** Wowhead Druid HC Leveling Guide — https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/classes/druid/hardcore-leveling-tips

Travel Form (no mount needed!) + Bear/Cat shape-shifting + self-heal = HC'nin en versatil class'ı.

## Özet
Druid = HC'nin "swiss army knife" class'ı. **4 form** (Bear tank, Cat DPS, Travel Form +40% speed, Aquatic Form swim), self-heal (Rejuvenation + Regrowth + Healing Touch), unique **mount yerine Travel Form** (gold tasarrufu). Feral leveling, Balance solo, Restoration dungeon healer.

---

## 1. Spec Seçimi (HC)

### 1.1 Feral (Cat/Bear, Solo Leveling) — **Önerilen 1-60**
- Cat Form DPS + Bear tank toggle.
- Mangle yok Classic'te; Shred (back attack) + Rake + Rip combo.

### 1.2 Balance (Caster Solo) — **Alternative**
- Wrath + Starfire spam; Moonkin Form (lvl 40 talent).
- HC riski: no mana sustain like Cat.

### 1.3 Restoration (Healer) — **Group/Endgame**
- Rejuvenation + Regrowth + Healing Touch.
- Dungeon healer optimal.

### 1.4 Önerilen HC Build (Feral Leveling)

- **Tier 1 Feral**: 5/5 Ferocity (energy/rage discount), 3/3 Feral Aggression.
- **Tier 2**: 5/5 Thick Hide (+armor in forms), 1/1 Feline Swiftness (+30% speed Cat).
- **Tier 3**: 1/1 Feral Charge, 3/3 Sharpened Claws.
- **Tier 4**: 1/1 Faerie Fire (Feral) (instant, ranged pull), 3/3 Predatory Strikes.
- **Tier 5**: 5/5 Heart of the Wild (Stamina/Strength/Int bonus).
- **Tier 6**: 1/1 Leader of the Pack (5% crit aura).
- **Tier 7**: 1/1 Mangle? No, that's Burning Crusade. Classic top: 1/1 Improved Leader of the Pack (5% self-heal on crit).

## 2. HC-Özel Strateji

### 2.1 Form Switching Discipline
- **Bear Form** (lvl 10) — tank, +180% armor, taunt (Growl), Demoralizing Roar.
- **Cat Form** (lvl 20) — DPS, Stealth (Prowl), Dash (+70% speed), Shred/Rake/Rip.
- **Travel Form** (lvl 30) — +40% speed; combat'a girince düşer.
- **Aquatic Form** (lvl 16) — swim speed +50%, no drowning.

### 2.2 Travel Form = Mount Replacement
- **Lvl 30** itibariyle Druid mount almaya gerek yok.
- **80g + riding skill bypassed.** HC gold tasarrufu büyük.
- Combat girince form düşer → 0.5 sn pre-drop sürat avantajı.

### 2.3 Self-Heal Discipline
- **Rejuvenation** (lvl 4) — HoT.
- **Regrowth** (lvl 12) — instant HoT + cast heal.
- **Healing Touch** (lvl 1) — big single heal.
- **Innervate** (Restoration lvl 40 talent) — mana refill 20 sn, **kendine cast**!
- Combat'tan çıkış: Rejuv + Regrowth + bandage.

### 2.4 Bear Survival
- **Frenzied Regeneration** (Bear, lvl 36) — convert rage to HP (10 HP per 1 rage).
- **Barkskin** (lvl 44) — -20% damage taken, **uninterruptable cast** while in form.
- **Bash** (Bear lvl 26) — 4 sec stun.

### 2.5 Cat Stealth (Prowl)
- **Lvl 20** — stealth in Cat Form.
- HC: skip patrols like Rogue.
- Pounce (Cat opener) — 4 sec stun.

### 2.6 Faerie Fire (Feral instant)
- **Lvl 20+** instant cast ranged pull (in form).
- Armor reduction debuff.

### 2.7 Hibernate (Lvl 18)
- Beast/Dragonkin CC, 40 sec sleep.

### 2.8 Innervate Self
- **Lvl 40 Restoration talent** — mana refill.
- HC: solo'da mana starvation çözer.

## 3. Stat Öncelikleri

### Feral Leveling (Cat)
1. **Strength** > **Agility** > **Stamina** > **Intellect**

### Bear Tank
1. **Stamina** > **Agility** (dodge) > **Strength** > **Defense skill**

### Balance
1. **Intellect** > **+Spell dmg** > **Spirit** > **Stamina**

### Restoration
1. **Intellect** > **Spirit** > **+Healing** > **Stamina**

## 4. Rotasyon

### Feral Cat Solo
```
Pre-pull: Faerie Fire (Feral) → Cat Form → Pounce (stealthed opener, 4 sec stun) →
Rake (DoT) → Shred (positional, back) → Rip (finisher 4-5 CP)
```

### Bear Tank
```
Faerie Fire → Bear Form → Maul (rage finisher) → Swipe (AoE 3 mobs) → Growl (taunt) →
Demoralizing Roar (-stat debuff) → Bash (stun emergency)
```

### Balance Caster
```
Faerie Fire → Wrath (spam) → Moonfire (DoT) → Starfire (high mana, big damage burst)
```

## 8. PvP / Mak'gora

### Strengths
- **Form shifting** = root/snare break.
- **Travel Form** kite.
- **Cat stealth** opener.
- **Hibernate** Beast/Dragonkin (rare).
- **Self-heal** Regrowth + Rejuv + HT.

### Weaknesses
- **Polymorph** removes form.
- **Slow ranged** if Resto.

### Mak'gora Tactics
- Open Cat Pounce → Rake → Shred → Rip finisher.
- Form shift dispel roots → Travel Form kite if losing.
- Bear Form Frenzied Regen for emergency HP convert.

---

## 9. HC-Spesifik Uyarılar

> **HC** Druid ölüm sebepleri:
> 1. **Form drop mid-combat** (Polymorph, stun).
> 2. **Mana starvation** (Cat to Resto switch).
> 3. **Travel Form combat drop** → ride lost, runs slow.
> 4. **Solo dungeon over-pull** (Bear tank multi-add).
> 5. **Hibernate breaks** on damage tick.

### Survival Priority
1. **Bear Form** for tank emergency.
2. **Cat Stealth (Prowl)** patrol skip.
3. **Self-heal stack** (Rejuv + Regrowth always).
4. **Travel Form** for escape (out of combat).
5. **Innervate** self for mana.
