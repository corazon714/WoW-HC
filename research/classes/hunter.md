# Hunter — Hardcore Rehberi (Tüm Spec'ler)

**Versiyon:** Classic Era 1.15.x / Anniversary HC (Classic Fresh)
**Son Güncelleme:** 2026-06-18
**Class Difficulty (HC):** **DÜŞÜK** — Wowhead HC Tier List: **A Tier**. HC'nin **en hızlı leveling** ve **en güvenli ranged** class'ı. Pet tank + Feign Death + Aspect of the Cheetah = ölmek için aktif olarak çabalamak gerekir.
**Birincil Kaynak:** Wowhead Hunter HC Leveling Guide — https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/classes/hunter/hardcore-leveling-tips

## Özet
Hunter = HC'nin meta class'ı. **Pet tank**, **Aspect of the Cheetah** (+30% speed), **Feign Death** (aggro reset), **Concussive Shot** (slow), **Frost Trap** (root) — tüm survival kit class kit içinde. Beast Mastery leveling, Marksman damage spike, Survival utility.

---

## 1. Spec Seçimi (HC)

### 1.1 Beast Mastery (Leveling) — **Önerilen 1-60**
- Pet damage + survival; pet bear/cat tank.
- Bestial Wrath (lvl 30) — pet rage damage burst.
- BM = HC's safest spec.

### 1.2 Marksmanship (Damage) — **40+ alternatif**
- Aimed Shot, Improved Hawk, ranged crit.
- HC için en yüksek burst damage ama pet zayıflar.

### 1.3 Survival (Trap/Crit) — **PvP/Utility**
- Wyvern Sting, Lacerate, Counterattack.
- Solo PvP odaklı; HC PvP yok.

### 1.4 Önerilen HC Spec Build (BM Leveling)

- **Tier 1 BM**: 5/5 Improved Aspect of the Hawk, 1/3 Endurance Training.
- **Tier 2 BM**: 5/5 Improved Revive Pet (HC için!), 3/3 Pathfinding (passive +%6 speed Travel Form'da).
- **Tier 3 BM**: 1/1 Bestial Swiftness (pet +30% speed), 2/5 Unleashed Fury.
- **Tier 4 BM**: 5/5 Improved Mend Pet, 1/1 Beast Within / Frenzy.
- **Tier 5+**: Spirit Bond (HoT for both), 1/1 Bestial Wrath.

## 2. HC-Özel Strateji

### 2.1 Pet = Tank
- Erkenden pet capture: **Boar** (Webwood Lurker for night elf? no, boar from Elwynn/Mulgore lvl 10+) — Charge (free rage stun) + Frenzy.
- **Cat** (Stranglethorn) — Dash speed.
- **Bear** (Hillsbrad/Loch Modan) — high HP, Growl tank.
- **Wind Serpent** (Hinterlands) — Lightning Breath ranged DPS.
- **Devilsaur** (Un'Goro) — endgame pet, Mortal Strike + Charge.

### 2.2 Aspect Management
- **Aspect of the Hawk** — damage boost; combat'ta.
- **Aspect of the Monkey** — dodge +8%; oh-shit defensive.
- **Aspect of the Cheetah** (lvl 16) — +30% speed but daze on hit; out-of-combat travel.
- **Aspect of the Pack** (lvl 20) — group +30% speed.
- **Aspect of the Wild** (lvl 30) — nature resist; vs Druid/elemental.

### 2.3 Feign Death — **HC #1 escape**
- **Lvl 30** — drop combat fake death.
- **%30 fail rate** (vanilla bug); always have backup.
- Combat pause exhilarates → Bandage, Hearth, Mount.

### 2.4 Trap Combo
- **Frost Trap** (lvl 20): area slow.
- **Freezing Trap** (lvl 20): single target freeze 15 sn.
- **Immolation Trap** (lvl 16): DoT.
- **Explosive Trap** (lvl 30): AoE damage.
- HC pull rotation: Freezing Trap → mob frozen → Concussive Shot → kite.

### 2.5 Concussive Shot — Kiting fundamentals
- Lvl 8 — 4 sn slow.
- Combine with Aspect of Cheetah → kite indefinitely.
- "Three-shot kite": Concussive Shot → run 5 yards → Auto Shot → repeat.

### 2.6 Disengage (Survival, lvl 20)
- 5 yard backward jump + threat reduction.
- Combo: aggro → Disengage → Feign Death.

### 2.7 Mend Pet — Pet survival
- Heal pet over time; cast while moving.
- Pet ölünce **15 dakika** Revive Pet cooldown (HC eğer pet öldü). **Pet ölmesin** = priority.
- Improved Revive Pet (BM talent) — instant cast + full HP rez.

## 4. Rotasyon

### Solo Pull
```
Hunter's Mark (lvl 6, +damage debuff) → Pet send (Aspect of Hawk) →
Auto Shot (start) → Arcane Shot (instant, every CD) → Multi-Shot (only safe mob spread) →
Serpent Sting (DoT, low rank for mana efficiency) → continue Auto Shot
```

### Kite Sequence
```
Concussive Shot → run 5 yards → Auto Shot → Concussive Shot (re-apply) → repeat
```

### Trap Setup
```
Freezing Trap (place) → Eyes of the Beast / aggro pull → mob hits trap → 15 sec CC →
turret-style damage
```

### Pet Tank (group dungeon)
- Growl on (pet tanking) — pet aggro mob.
- Mend Pet maintained.
- Hunter ranged DPS from back.

## 8. PvP / Mak'gora

### Strengths
- **Kiting** master (Concussive + Cheetah).
- **Freezing Trap** opener CC.
- **Scatter Shot** (lvl 30) — 4 sn disorient.
- **Feign Death** combat reset.

### Weaknesses
- **Caster opener** — Polymorph (Mage) opener instant kill chance.
- **No melee** in dead zone (5 yard); melee opener bad.

### Mak'gora Tactics
- Open from 35 yards: Aimed Shot → Concussive → trap → kite.
- Free Action Potion before duel.
- Pet send first to absorb opening damage.

## 9. HC-Spesifik Uyarılar

> **HC** Hunter ölüm sebepleri:
> 1. **Pet ölmesi** ve Hunter solo'ya geçmesi (multi-add).
> 2. **Feign Death %30 fail** ve ölüm.
> 3. **Multi-mob pull** trap olmadan.
> 4. **Dead zone** (5 yard) içinde mob melee.
> 5. **Disconnect** — pet tank tutarken DC.

### Survival Priority
1. **Pet alive** = #1 priority. Mend Pet maintain.
2. **Feign Death backup** (Hearthstone / Disengage).
3. **Aspect of Cheetah** out of combat (combat'ta Hawk).
4. **Trap mid-fight** combat-drop combo.
