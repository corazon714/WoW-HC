# Priest — Hardcore Rehberi (Tüm Spec'ler)

**Versiyon:** Classic Era 1.15.x / Anniversary HC (Classic Fresh)
**Son Güncelleme:** 2026-06-18
**Class Difficulty (HC):** **ORTA** — Wowhead HC Tier List: **B Tier** (solo); endgame'de **Dwarf Priest = Alliance HC raid'de yeri tartisilmaz** (Fear Ward exclusive). Power Word: Shield + Renew + Fade ile güvenli ama düşük damage = uzun fight.
**Birincil Kaynak:** Wowhead Priest HC Leveling Guide — https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/classes/priest/hardcore-leveling-tips

## Özet
Priest = HC'nin **destek master'ı**. Power Word: Shield (PWS) ile damage absorb, Renew ile HoT, Fade ile threat dump. Solo leveling Shadow tercihli (DoT damage + Vampiric Embrace heal). Endgame Holy dungeon healer.

---

## 1. Spec Seçimi (HC)

### 1.1 Shadow (Solo Leveling) — **Önerilen 1-60**
- Vampiric Embrace (lvl 30 talent) = %20 damage as self-heal.
- Shadowform (lvl 40) — %15 dmg boost but no healing.
- Mind Flay (Shadow lvl 20) — sustained damage + slow.

### 1.2 Holy (Healer) — **Group/Endgame**
- Spiritual Guidance, Improved Healing, Spiritual Healing.
- Solo zayıf; dungeon healer için optimal.

### 1.3 Discipline (Utility) — **Hybrid**
- Power Infusion (lvl 40 talent) — buff ally.
- Hybrid Disc/Holy build endgame.

### 1.4 Önerilen HC Build (Shadow Leveling)

- **Tier 1 Shadow**: 5/5 Spirit Tap, 3/3 Blackout.
- **Tier 2**: 3/3 Improved Shadow Word: Pain, 1/1 Mind Flay.
- **Tier 3**: 5/5 Improved Mind Blast, 1/1 Vampiric Embrace.
- **Tier 4**: 5/5 Improved Shadowform, 2/3 Shadow Reach.
- **Tier 5+**: Silence (1/1), Darkness (5/5).

> **Lvl 30**: 1/1 **Vampiric Embrace** — HC Shadow Priest signature.

## 2. HC-Özel Strateji

### 2.1 Power Word: Shield (PWS)
- **Lvl 6+**: absorbs damage.
- **Weakened Soul debuff 15 sec** → can't re-shield until expired.
- HC: shield before every pull.

### 2.2 Vampiric Embrace (Shadow Lvl 30 talent)
- %20 damage → self-heal as Shadow priest. **Solo sustain için altın**.
- Refresh every fight.

### 2.3 Mind Soothe — Patrol skip
- **Lvl 8** — reduce mob aggro radius.
- Sneak past humanoid patrols.

### 2.4 Fade — Threat dump
- **Lvl 8** — threat reduction 10 sec; in groups crucial.
- HC: fade-bandage combo.

### 2.5 Psychic Scream (Fear, lvl 8)
- 5 yard fear AoE; **fear-into-pack** risk yüksek.
- Sadece açık alanda kullan; dungeon içinde tehlikeli.

### 2.6 Shackle Undead — Undead CC
- **Lvl 20** — undead-only sleep; Scholomance/Strat dungeon CC.

### 2.7 Dispel Magic + Cure Disease + Abolish Disease
- Self + party utility.

### 2.8 Healing Cycle (Solo Shadow)
- Vampiric Embrace tick = sustained heal.
- Bandage post-combat (First Aid).
- **Greater Heal** (lvl 40) — big single heal.
- **Flash Heal** (lvl 20) — instant-ish (1.5 sec cast).

### 2.9 Shadowform Discipline
- **Lvl 40**: +15% Shadow dmg, -15% physical dmg taken. Cannot cast Holy spells in form.
- **HC risk**: cannot heal yourself in Shadowform → drop form for emergency heal.
- Macro:
  ```
  /cancelaura Shadowform
  /cast Flash Heal
  ```

## 4. Rotasyon

### Shadow Solo
```
PW:Shield → Shadow Word: Pain (DoT) → Mind Blast → Mind Flay → Vampiric Embrace (apply once) →
weave Mind Flay + SW:Pain refresh → at low HP: drop Shadowform → Flash Heal → re-Shadowform
```

### Holy Healing
- Renew always rolling.
- PWS pre-pull.
- Greater Heal for spike.
- Flash Heal for emergency.

## 8. PvP / Mak'gora

### Strengths
- **Shield + Renew + Heal** = endless sustain.
- **Fear** opener (Psychic Scream).
- **Silence** caster interrupt.
- **Mind Control** (lvl 30) — turn opponent into your puppet.

### Weaknesses
- **No instant burst** — slow kills.
- **Slow death** = energy fight.

### Mak'gora Tactics
- PWS → SWP → MB → MF → Psychic Scream (fear away from packs) → heal up → repeat.
- Free Action Potion before duel.

---

## 9. HC-Spesifik Uyarılar

> **HC** Priest ölüm sebepleri:
> 1. **Shadowform stuck** — can't heal self.
> 2. **Fear-into-pack** — Psychic Scream fled mob aggro.
> 3. **Mana starvation** uzun fight (Shadow Word: Pain spam).
> 4. **Mob silence** during cast (Library SM).

### Survival Priority
1. **PWS pre-pull** her zaman.
2. **Vampiric Embrace apply** her fight.
3. **Drop Shadowform** before emergency heal.
4. **Fade + Bandage** mid-combat.
