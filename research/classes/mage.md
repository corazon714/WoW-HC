# Mage — Hardcore Rehberi (Tüm Spec'ler)

**Versiyon:** Classic Era 1.15.x / Anniversary HC (Classic Fresh)
**Son Güncelleme:** 2026-06-18
**Class Difficulty (HC):** **DÜŞÜK-ORTA** — Wowhead HC Tier List: **S Tier** (Paladin, Rogue ile birlikte zirvede). Frost spec = HC'nin meta class'larından biri. Frost Nova + Blink + Polymorph = neredeyse her tehlike çıkar.
**Birincil Kaynak:** Wowhead Mage HC Leveling Guide — https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/classes/mage/hardcore-leveling-tips

## Özet
Mage = HC'nin "control master"ı. **Frost Nova** (8 sec root AoE), **Blink** (20 yard teleport), **Polymorph** (long sheep), **Frost Ward + Ice Block** survival kit. Frost leveling AoE Frostbolt + Frost Nova combo, Arcane Power burst, Fire (AoE riskli — fire vulnerability dungeons).

---

## 1. Spec Seçimi (HC)

### 1.1 Frost (Leveling) — **Önerilen 1-60**
- Frost Nova + Frostbolt slow + Blink + Cold Snap (CD reset) = control god.
- AoE pull capable with Improved Blizzard + Permafrost.

### 1.2 Fire (Damage) — **40+ alternatif**
- Pyroblast + Combustion + Critical Mass.
- HC riski: fire immune mobs (BRS, BRD), AoE blowback.

### 1.3 Arcane (Utility/Spec) — **Niche**
- Arcane Power, Presence of Mind (instant cast), Evocation.
- Solo zayıf damage; utility hybrid.

### 1.4 Önerilen HC Build (Frost Leveling)

- **Tier 1 Frost**: 5/5 Frost Warding, 3/3 Improved Frostbolt.
- **Tier 2**: 3/3 Ice Shards, 5/5 Elemental Precision.
- **Tier 3**: 3/3 Permafrost (slow), 1/1 Cold Snap.
- **Tier 4**: 5/5 Improved Blizzard, 3/3 Arctic Reach.
- **Tier 5**: 1/1 Ice Barrier (HC altın — absorb shield).
- **Tier 6**: 1/1 Ice Block (full immunity).
- **Tier 7**: 1/1 Winter's Chill (debuff for raid).

> **Lvl 30**: 1/1 **Cold Snap** — Frost spell CD reset, HC kritik.
> **Lvl 40**: 1/1 **Ice Block** — 10 sec full immunity, **HC Mage signature**.

## 2. HC-Özel Strateji

### 2.1 Frost Nova → Blink Combo
- **Frost Nova** (lvl 10) — 8 sec area root, no damage immunity.
- **Blink** (lvl 16) — 20 yard teleport, breaks roots/snares.
- Combo: Frost Nova → Blink → kite distance → repeat.

### 2.2 Polymorph (Sheep) — **HC #1 CC**
- **Lvl 8** — 50 sec sheep humanoid/beast.
- Pack pulled? Sheep one, kill other.
- HC pull discipline: **always sheep before AoE engage**.

### 2.3 Ice Block (Frost 40 talent)
- 10 sec full damage immunity.
- **Cannot attack/cast** during.
- Combo: low HP → Ice Block → Bandage / Hearth.

### 2.4 Cold Snap (Frost 30 talent)
- Resets all Frost spell CDs.
- Stack: Frost Nova → Blink → Cold Snap → Frost Nova → Blink (double escape).

### 2.5 Mana Shield + Evocation
- **Mana Shield** (lvl 14) — convert mana to damage absorb.
- **Evocation** (lvl 20) — channel 8 sec, restore 60% mana.
- Combat'ta Evocation kullanma (interrupt risk); after combat for refill.

### 2.6 Conjure Food + Water
- **Lvl 6+** Conjure Water, **Lvl 10+** Conjure Food.
- Saves food/water budget. **Self-found HC için critical.**

### 2.7 Invisibility (Lvl 30 Arcane talent)
- 10 sec fade + 10 sec invis = 20 sec total.
- Aggro reset + travel safety.
- Less reliable than Rogue stealth but works.

### 2.8 Mage Portals (Lvl 40)
- **Group only** — portal serves group members.
- Solo Mage cannot teleport another player.
- **Self-teleport** (lvl 20) instant; HS bypass.

## 4. Rotasyon

### Frost Solo
```
Polymorph (sheep if pack) → Frostbolt (3 sec cast initial) → Frostbolt spam →
mob in melee range: Frost Nova → Blink back → Frostbolt → finish
```

### Multi-Target (3+ mobs)
```
Polymorph (one) → Frost Nova (AoE root) → Blink → Cone of Cold (AoE damage) →
Blizzard channel (or Arcane Explosion if melee range) → kite
```

### Emergency
```
Low HP → Ice Block → 10 sec safe → Bandage 8 sec → Hearth 10 sec (start in IB final sec)
```

## 8. PvP / Mak'gora

### Strengths
- **Polymorph** opener kontrol.
- **Frost Nova + Blink + Cold Snap + Frost Nova + Blink** chain escape.
- **Ice Block** survival 10 sn.
- **Counterspell** healer/caster lock.

### Weaknesses
- **Cloth armor** = melee burst kills.
- **Slow opener** caster (3 sec Frostbolt).

### Mak'gora Tactics
- Open Polymorph (45 sec) → Frostbolt spam from 30 yards → Frost Nova when melee close → Blink.
- Cold Snap → repeat Nova.
- Ice Block at low HP → resume.

## 9. HC-Spesifik Uyarılar

> **HC** Mage ölüm sebepleri:
> 1. **Cloth armor melee burst** (gnoll mob 2-3 hits).
> 2. **Counterspell mid-cast** (caster mob).
> 3. **Polymorph sheep break** — DoT (Pain) on sheep target = polymorph broken.
> 4. **Fire vulnerability** (BRS, BRD) — Fire Mage dead.
> 5. **Evocation interrupt** — mid-channel mob hit = mana wasted.

### Survival Priority
1. **Polymorph EVERY humanoid extra.**
2. **Frost Nova + Blink** ready always.
3. **Ice Block** for emergency.
4. **Mana Shield** + Evocation between fights.
5. **Cone of Cold** for melee burst escape.
