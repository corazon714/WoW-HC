# Rogue — Hardcore Rehberi (Tüm Spec'ler)

**Versiyon:** Classic Era 1.15.x / Anniversary HC (Classic Fresh)
**Son Güncelleme:** 2026-06-18
**Class Difficulty (HC):** **ORTA** — Wowhead HC Tier List: **S Tier** (Mage, Paladin ile birlikte). Stealth + Vanish + Sprint güçlü survival, ama no self-heal + close-range = pull yanlış olursa ölüm.
**Birincil Kaynak:** Wowhead Rogue HC Leveling Guide — https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/classes/rogue/hardcore-leveling-tips

## Özet
Rogue HC'de stealth-based avoidance master'ı. **Stealth** ile patrol skip, **Sap** ile pack CC, **Vanish** ile combat reset, **Sprint** ile kaçış. Ana zorluk: damage burst için pozisyon, mana yerine energy/CP rotasyon, ve Mage gibi instant heal yok.

---

## 1. Spec Seçimi (HC)

### 1.1 Combat Swords/Maces (Leveling) — **Önerilen 1-60**
- Sword Spec / Mace Spec procs; sustained DPS.
- Adrenaline Rush (lvl 40 Combat talent) — burst.
- HC önerisi: **Combat Swords**.

### 1.2 Assassination (Daggers) — **Backstab spec**
- Backstab + Imp Eviscerate; positional but high crit.
- Solo leveling iyi ama dagger weapons sınırlı.

### 1.3 Subtlety (Stealth/Utility) — **PvP only**
- Premeditation, Preparation (Vanish + Sprint reset).
- Solo PvE zayıf; HC için gereksiz.

### 1.4 Önerilen HC Build (Combat Swords Leveling)

- **Tier 1 Combat**: 5/5 Improved Sinister Strike, 5/5 Lightning Reflexes.
- **Tier 2**: 5/5 Improved Backstab (Combat Daggers route), 5/5 Deflection.
- **Tier 3**: 5/5 Precision (hit %), 1/1 Riposte.
- **Tier 4**: 5/5 Dual Wield Specialization.
- **Tier 5**: 5/5 Sword Specialization (1H sword bonus chance to attack again).
- **Tier 6**: 1/1 Blade Flurry (AoE swing).
- **Tier 7**: 1/1 Adrenaline Rush.

## 2. HC-Özel Strateji

### 2.1 Stealth Discipline
- **Stealth always on** out of combat (lvl 1+).
- **Stealth detection radius** = (your level - mob level). Higher mob detection level → stealth break.
- **Crouch (Combat Pose Stealth)** = -70% movement speed.
- **Master of Deception** talent (Subtlety) — stealth detection radius bonus.

### 2.2 Sap → Stealth → Pick → Vanish chain
- **Sap** (lvl 14) — 45 sec humanoid CC; out of combat only.
- Sap patrol → walk past → re-Stealth → continue.
- HC patrol skip = Rogue's killer feature.

### 2.3 Vanish — **HC #1 escape**
- **Lvl 22**: 10 sec invisibility; combat exit + threat reset.
- **CD 5 dakika** (Subtlety Preparation reset).
- Vanish → Stealth → Sprint → safe distance.

### 2.4 Sprint
- **Lvl 8**: +70% movement 15 sec, **CD 5 dakika** (Subtlety Preparation reset).
- Escape combo: Vanish → Sprint → Hearth.

### 2.5 Blind (Lvl 56)
- 10 sec disorient; **CC anything**; reagent: Blinding Powder (vendor).
- Late-game emergency.

### 2.6 Combat Stance Tricks
- **Riposte** (lvl 20 Combat talent): after parry, instant strike + 4 sec disarm.
- **Evasion** (lvl 8): +50% dodge 15 sec.
- **Feint** (lvl 16): threat reduction.

### 2.7 Energy Management
- 100 energy max; +20 per 2 sec.
- Sinister Strike 45 energy → 5 CP → Eviscerate (35 energy).
- HC: never pool to 100 then dump; consistent SS.

## 4. Rotasyon

### Solo Pull (Combat Swords)
```
Stealth → Cheap Shot (4 sec stun, opener) → Sinister Strike x3 (gain 3 CP) →
Kidney Shot (5 sec stun) → Sinister Strike x2 (2 CP) → Eviscerate (finish)
```

### Backstab (Daggers)
```
Stealth → Ambush (high damage opener) → Backstab x3 → Slice and Dice (5 CP, attack speed) →
SS or Backstab spam → Eviscerate (finish)
```

### Group Dungeon
- Tank pulls → Rogue applies poisons → SS damage → Kidney Shot on caster interrupt → Eviscerate.
- Sap second mob before pull (CC).

### AoE (Blade Flurry)
- Adrenaline Rush + Blade Flurry → SS spam → 2 mobs cleaved.

## 8. PvP / Mak'gora

### Strengths
- **Stealth opener** instant kill setup.
- **Cheap Shot + Kidney Shot** stunlock chain.
- **Vanish** combat reset.
- **Sap** out of combat CC.

### Weaknesses
- **No self-heal** — long fight bad.
- **AoE detection** (Mage Detect Magic, Hunter Flare).
- **Stunlocked target wakes** with PvP trinket / Will of the Forsaken.

### Mak'gora Tactics
- Open with **Ambush + Cheap Shot** + max CP → **Kidney Shot** → Eviscerate burst.
- Free Action Potion before duel (anti-fear).
- Vanish if low HP → re-stealth → re-engage.

## 9. HC-Spesifik Uyarılar

> **HC** Rogue ölüm sebepleri:
> 1. **Stealth break mid-pull** — high level mob detection.
> 2. **Vanish %5 fail rate** — debuff active vanishedde fail.
> 3. **Energy starvation** — combat opening wrong energy.
> 4. **AoE detection mob** (Mage demon, Hunter pet).
> 5. **No self-heal** post-combat = bandage uzun süre.

### Survival Priority
1. **Stealth out of combat** her zaman.
2. **Sap before patrol approach.**
3. **Vanish CD ready** every dangerous pull.
4. **Bandage post-combat** her zaman.
5. **Healing potion** combat HP <50%.
