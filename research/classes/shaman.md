# Shaman — Hardcore Rehberi (Horde Only, Tüm Spec'ler)

**Versiyon:** Classic Era 1.15.x / Anniversary HC (Classic Fresh)
**Son Güncelleme:** 2026-06-18
**Class Difficulty (HC):** **ORTA** — Wowhead HC Tier List: **B Tier** (solo leveling için). Grupta Windfury/Tremor totem sinerjisi ile S tier'a yakın; solo "lackluster".
**Birincil Kaynak:** Wowhead Shaman HC Leveling Guide — https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/classes/shaman/hardcore-leveling-tips

> **🚨 KRİTİK UYARI — Classic Fresh HC**
> 
> **Reincarnation (self-rez) Classic Fresh HC realmlerinde DEVRE DIŞIDIR.** Wowhead resmi Overview'in "Cheat Death Mechanics Removed" bölümünden:
> > "Shamans cannot use Reincarnation."
> 
> Yani Shaman'ın efsanevi self-rez özelliği (lvl 30 class quest, Ankh reagent) Classic Fresh HC'de **çalışmaz**. Realm bazlı tüm rezurrection disable.
> 
> - **Original HC (2023 realmleri):** Çalışıyor (1.15.5 patch'inden sonra 5 sn post-rez invuln eklendi).
> - **Classic Fresh HC (2024+):** KAPALI.
> 
> Pratik sonuç: Shaman'ın Classic Fresh'te survival tool kit'i Ghost Wolf + Tremor Totem + Healing Wave + Frost Shock kite + Astral Recall ile sınırlı. Reincarnation'a güvenmeyin.

## Özet
Shaman = HC'nin orta zorlukta class'ı. **Ghost Wolf** (+40% speed, combat'ta kullanılabilir), **Healing Wave + Lesser Healing Wave**, **Astral Recall** (HS alternatif), **Tremor Totem** (fear immunity). Enhancement leveling, Elemental burst, Restoration dungeon healer. Endgame raid'de Horde'un raid meta omurgası — Windfury/Mana Spring/Tranquil Air totem twisting + Tremor (Horde'un Fear Ward muadili).

---

## 1. Spec Seçimi (HC)

### 1.1 Enhancement (Melee, Solo) — **Önerilen 1-60**
- 2H weapon + Windfury Weapon (1H/2H imbue, lvl 30 talent).
- Stormstrike (lvl 40 talent) — nature dmg burst.
- Wolf form for travel.

### 1.2 Elemental (Caster) — **Solo alternatif**
- Lightning Bolt + Earth Shock + Frost Shock.
- HC riski: mana starvation, no melee defense.

### 1.3 Restoration (Healer) — **Group/Endgame**
- Healing Wave + Lesser Healing Wave + Chain Heal.

### 1.4 Önerilen HC Build (Enhancement Leveling)

- **Tier 1 Enhancement**: 5/5 Ancestral Knowledge (+5% mana), 5/5 Shield Specialization (Shaman shield use).
- **Tier 2**: 3/3 Guardian Totems, 5/5 Two-Handed Axes and Maces.
- **Tier 3**: 5/5 Anticipation (+5% dodge), 2/3 Flurry.
- **Tier 4**: 1/1 Parry, 3/3 Improved Lightning Shield.
- **Tier 5**: 5/5 Weapon Mastery.
- **Tier 6**: 1/1 Stormstrike.
- **Tier 7**: 1/1 Improved Stormstrike, 3/3 Elemental Weapons.

## 2. HC-Özel Strateji

### 2.1 Reincarnation — **Sadece Original HC'de çalışır**
- **Lvl 30 quest** ("Call of Earth" zinciri ile açılır) class quest.
- **60 minute CD**, item-based (Ankh reagent, vendor cost).
- Self-rez at death; 20% HP/mana, **5 sec invuln post-rez** (1.15.5 buff — Original HC için).
- **⚠️ Classic Fresh HC'de DEVRE DIŞI — Wowhead Overview:** "Shamans cannot use Reincarnation."
- **Original HC'de** klasik Shaman save — Ankh stoku tükenmemeli, CD aktifken extra dikkatli oyna.
- **Classic Fresh'te** Reincarnation büyüsü kitapta var ama HC realm flag'ı rez'i kapatıyor; spell cast olsa bile karakter yaşamaz.

### 2.2 Ghost Wolf (Lvl 20)
- **+40% movement speed**, can be used in combat (unlike Druid Travel Form).
- HC kite / escape primary.
- Imp Ghost Wolf (talent) — instant cast.

### 2.3 Totem Discipline
- **Earthbind Totem** (lvl 6) — AoE slow; HC kite essential.
- **Stoneskin Totem** — +physical armor.
- **Strength of Earth Totem** — Strength buff.
- **Healing Stream Totem** — passive heal.
- **Searing Totem** — passive DPS.
- **Mana Spring Totem** — mana regen.
- **Magma Totem** (lvl 22+) — AoE damage.
- **Fire Resistance Totem** — vs fire mob.
- **Tremor Totem** — fear/charm immunity radius (HC ALTIN vs Warlock/mob fear).
- **Frost Resistance Totem** — vs frost mob.
- **Grounding Totem** (lvl 50) — absorbs next spell cast at it.

### 2.4 Astral Recall (Lvl 30)
- Hearthstone alternative; **15 minute CD separate from HS**.
- HC: **2 HS instead of 1**.
- Bind to important hub (Crossroads / Orgrimmar / Thunder Bluff).

### 2.5 Self-Heal
- **Healing Wave** (lvl 1) — big single heal.
- **Lesser Healing Wave** (lvl 20) — fast/cheap.
- **Earth Shield** (TBC — not in Classic).
- **Chain Heal** (lvl 40 Restoration) — group heal.

### 2.6 Frost Shock (Slow)
- **Lvl 20** — single target snare 8 sec.
- Kite primary.

### 2.7 Earth Shock (Interrupt)
- **Lvl 4** — silence cast 2 sec.

### 2.8 Wind Shock? No — that's TBC.

### 2.9 Stoneclaw Totem
- Decoy mob; pulls aggro from Shaman.
- HC emergency.

## 4. Rotasyon

### Enhancement Solo
```
Stoneskin Totem → Searing Totem → Lightning Shield (buff) →
Pull: Lightning Bolt (ranged opener) → 2H attack → Earth Shock (rage when caster) →
Stormstrike (lvl 40 talent) → Frost Shock (low HP escape) → Ghost Wolf kite
```

### Elemental
```
Lightning Shield → Searing Totem → Lightning Bolt spam → Earth Shock (instant) →
Frost Shock (slow kite) → Mana Spring Totem (regen)
```

## 8. PvP / Duel to the Death (DTTD)

### Strengths
- **Ghost Wolf** kite (+40% speed, combat'ta kullanılabilir).
- **Tremor Totem** fear immunity — Warlock'a karşı eşitleyici.
- **Grounding Totem** spell absorb — caster opener'ını alır.
- **Frost Shock** kite slow.
- **Windfury Weapon** burst proc.
- **Reincarnation** (Original HC) — Classic Fresh'te DTTD'de zaten kazanan yaşadığı için Reincarnation kullanılamayacak (rez disable).

### Weaknesses
- **No interrupt** outside Earth Shock (silence 2 sec).
- **Mana intensive**.
- **Plate yok** — mail armor, melee burst'e zayıf.

### DTTD Tactics
- Open Lightning Bolt → close → Windfury procs → Stormstrike → Frost Shock if escape needed.
- Drop Tremor Totem if vs Warlock.
- Grounding Totem if vs Mage.

## 9. HC-Spesifik Uyarılar

> **HC** Shaman ölüm sebepleri:
> 1. **Classic Fresh'te Reincarnation'a güvenmek** — "Ankh'ım var, yaşarım" zihniyetiyle agresif oynayan oyuncu ilk ciddi hatada ölür; rez çalışmaz.
> 2. **Reincarnation reagent (Ankh) unutmak** (Original HC) — self-rez fail.
> 3. **Mana starvation** caster style.
> 4. **Totem misplacement** — fight'tan uzakta totem, etkisi yok.
> 5. **Ghost Wolf cast interrupt** mid-fight (1.5 sn cast, Improved talent ile instant).
> 6. **Mail armor** — plate yok, melee burst kırıcı.

### Survival Priority (Classic Fresh — Reincarnation YOK)
1. **Tremor Totem** ön hazırlık vs fear mob veya WoW HC "Defias Pillager"-class casters.
2. **Ghost Wolf** combat escape (Improved talent ile instant).
3. **Healing Wave** spike heal, **Lesser Healing Wave** sustained.
4. **Astral Recall + Hearthstone** = 2 emergency teleport.
5. **Grounding Totem** kaçınılmaz spell'i emer (lvl 50).
6. **Stoneclaw Totem** decoy — mob'u distract eder, kaçış penceresi açar.
7. **Earthbind Totem** AoE slow — multi-mob kaçış.

### Survival Priority (Original HC — Reincarnation AKTİF)
1. **Reincarnation Ankh** stoğunda her zaman (vendor: Reagent vendor, örn. Bargol Darkmane Orgrimmar).
2. **Tremor Totem** vs fear mob.
3. **Ghost Wolf** combat escape.
4. **Healing Wave** post-combat.
5. **Astral Recall + Hearthstone** = 2 emergency teleport.
