# Warlock — Hardcore Rehberi (Tüm Spec'ler)

**Versiyon:** Classic Era 1.15.x / Anniversary HC (Classic Fresh)
**Son Güncelleme:** 2026-06-18
**Class Difficulty (HC):** **DÜŞÜK** — Wowhead HC Tier List: **A Tier** (Hunter, Druid ile birlikte).
**Birincil Kaynak:** Wowhead Warlock HC Leveling Guide — https://www.wowhead.com/classic/guide/classes/warlock/hardcore-leveling-tips

> **🚨 KRİTİK UYARI — Classic Fresh HC**
> 
> **Soulstone (self-rez ve party-rez) Classic Fresh HC realmlerinde DEVRE DIŞIDIR.** Wowhead resmi Overview'in "Cheat Death Mechanics Removed" bölümünden:
> > "Warlocks cannot use a Soulstone or apply it to other players."
> 
> Yani Warlock'un imzası self-rez tool'u (lvl 18+ Soulstone item) Classic Fresh HC'de **hiç çalışmaz** — ne kendinize ne grup arkadaşınıza buff koyamazsınız (veya koyabilirsiniz ama rez tetiklendiğinde işlem reddedilir).
> 
> - **Original HC (2023 realmleri):** Çalışıyor. 1.15.5 patch'inden sonra rez sonrası 5 sn invuln eklendi.
> - **Classic Fresh HC (2024+):** KAPALI.
> 
> Pratik sonuç: Classic Fresh'te Warlock'un survival kit'i = Pet (Voidwalker tank) + Fear + Howl of Terror + Death Coil + Drain Life + Healthstone. Soulstone insurance artık yok.

## Özet
Warlock = HC'nin en sürdürülebilir DoT class'ı. **Voidwalker** tank, **Fear** kontrol, **Drain Life + Siphon Life** sustain, **Healthstone** party heal. Affliction leveling, Demonology pet tank, Destruction burst (riskli). Classic Fresh'te Soulstone insurance kaldırıldığı için dikkatli oynama daha kritik. Endgame'de Warlock raid'de Shadow Bolt spam ve Curse of Recklessness/Shadow utility ile yer alır.

## 1. Spec Seçimi (HC)

### 1.1 Affliction (Solo Leveling) — **Önerilen 1-60**
- DoTs + Drain Life sustain.
- Siphon Life (lvl 30 talent) — passive +heal from DoT.
- Curse spam.

### 1.2 Demonology (Pet Focus) — **Hybrid alternatif**
- Improved Voidwalker, Demonic Embrace (+15% stam).
- Sacrifice (lvl 50 talent) — pet sacrifice for shield.

### 1.3 Destruction (Burst) — **Risky 40+**
- Conflagrate, Devastation, Pyroclasm.
- HC riski: no sustain, burn out fast.

### 1.4 Önerilen HC Build (Affliction Leveling)

- **Tier 1 Affliction**: 5/5 Suppression, 5/5 Improved Corruption.
- **Tier 2**: 5/5 Improved Drain Life, 2/3 Improved Curse of Agony.
- **Tier 3**: 3/3 Improved Drain Soul, 5/5 Fel Concentration.
- **Tier 4**: 1/1 Amplify Curse, 3/3 Shadow Mastery.
- **Tier 5**: 1/1 Siphon Life (lvl 30 talent).
- **Tier 6**: 1/1 Dark Pact (mana refill from pet).
- **Tier 7**: 1/1 Curse of Exhaustion (slow), 1/1 Unstable Affliction (lvl 40).

> **Lvl 30**: **Siphon Life** — passive sustain.
> **Lvl 50**: 1/1 **Soul Link** (Demonology) — %30 dmg redirect to pet.

## 2. HC-Özel Strateji

### 2.1 Pet Discipline
- **Imp** (lvl 1) — ranged caster, low HP.
- **Voidwalker** (lvl 10) — tank, Sacrifice (shield), Suffering (taunt).
- **Succubus** (lvl 20) — DPS + Seduction (CC humanoid).
- **Felhunter** (lvl 30) — magic dispel, Spell Lock interrupt.
- HC: **Voidwalker leveling tank** until lvl 30+, then situational.

### 2.2 Soulstone — **Sadece Original HC'de çalışır**
- **Lvl 18+** — pre-place on self, self-rez on death.
- **30 minute CD** (Soulstone item CD).
- Soulshard reagent gerekli (1 şart).
- **⚠️ Classic Fresh HC'de DEVRE DIŞI — Wowhead Overview:** "Warlocks cannot use a Soulstone or apply it to other players."
- **Original HC'de** Soulstone rez sonrası %20 HP/mana + 5 sn invuln (1.15.5+) → vanish-out time.
- **Classic Fresh'te** Soulstone item kullanmaya çalışmak hata verir (veya buff cast olur ama rez devreye girmez); plan b yok, prevention'a güven.

### 2.3 Healthstone
- Conjured item — group HP potion.
- Cooldown party-wide; her HC karakter Healthstone bağımlı (party kullanırsa).

### 2.4 Fear (Lvl 8)
- 10 sec fear, 1 sec cast.
- **Fear-into-pack** ölümcül; openish area only.
- Improved Fear (talent) — bonus shadow dmg on fear end.

### 2.5 Drain Life — Sustain
- **Lvl 14** — channel; deals shadow dmg + heals self.
- HC: combat sustain self-heal.

### 2.6 Howl of Terror (Lvl 40 Affliction talent)
- 10 sec AoE fear; pack escape.
- Reset combat or escape.

### 2.7 Death Coil
- **Lvl 42** — 3 sec horror + dmg + self-heal.
- Emergency.

### 2.8 Curse of Exhaustion (Affliction talent)
- Slow target -50% movement.
- Kite spec.

### 2.9 Demonic Sacrifice (Demonology Lvl 50)
- Pet sacrificed → buff Warlock.
- Voidwalker sac = +20% Stamina; Imp sac = +15% Fire damage.

### 2.10 Death Coil + Fear + Howl of Terror combo
- 3 layered CC for escape; HC kit.

## 4. Rotasyon

### Affliction Solo
```
Pet send (Voidwalker, Suffering) → Curse of Agony → Corruption (DoT) → Drain Life (sustain) →
Shadow Bolt (filler) → repeat. Re-apply DoTs as expire.
```

### Burst (Destruction)
```
Shadow Bolt → Immolate → Conflagrate (consume Immolate) → Shadow Bolt spam
```

### Multi-Mob
```
Curse of Agony on all → Corruption on all → Fear primary → Drain Life on focus →
finish secondaries with Shadow Bolt
```

## 8. PvP / Duel to the Death (DTTD)

### Strengths
- **Fear** chain.
- **Drain Life** sustain.
- **Curse of Exhaustion** kite.
- **Howl of Terror** AoE fear.
- **Death Coil** 3 sn horror + heal.
- **Soulstone:** Original HC'de DTTD'de zaten geçerli değil (duel rule kullanılmaz); Classic Fresh'te ise rez zaten kapalı.

### Weaknesses
- **Caster** — interruptible (Kick, Counterspell, Earth Shock vs).
- **Pet killable** (Voidwalker focus down).
- **Cloth armor** — melee burst kırıcı.

### DTTD Tactics
- Open: Curse of Agony + Corruption + Immolate (DoTs) → Fear → Drain Life sustain.
- Death Coil interrupt opponent caster.
- Howl of Terror at low HP for reset window.
- Voidwalker Sacrifice (lvl 50 Demo talent) — +stamina shield burst.

## 9. HC-Spesifik Uyarılar

> **HC** Warlock ölüm sebepleri:
> 1. **Classic Fresh'te Soulstone'a güvenmek** — "Soulstone'um var, yaşarım" zihniyeti ölümcül. Soulstone Classic Fresh'te kapalı.
> 2. **Fear-into-pack** — fear yanlış yöne giderse Warlock'a yeni mob'lar getirir. Açık alan zorunlu.
> 3. **Pet dies, Lock solo** — multi-add wipe.
> 4. **Mana starvation** Drain Life spam.
> 5. **Cloth armor** melee burst kırıcı.
> 6. **Soulstone rez immediate death** (Original HC, pre-1.15.5 — artık 5 sn invuln var).

### Survival Priority (Classic Fresh — Soulstone YOK)
1. **Pet alive** = absolute priority. Voidwalker'ı her zaman damage soak edecek pozisyonda tut. Pet ölürse, hızlıca yeni summon (Soul Shard + 10 sn).
2. **Fear** sadece açık alanda, etrafa mob baktıktan sonra. Mob fear yoönünde respawn varsa çekme.
3. **Howl of Terror** (lvl 40 Affliction talent) AoE fear emergency reset.
4. **Healthstone** ready (5 min CD).
5. **Death Coil** emergency heal + horror.
6. **Drain Life** sustain when safe.
7. **Curse of Exhaustion** (talent) kite slow.
8. **Hearthstone preset** safe hub'a bağlı.

### Survival Priority (Original HC — Soulstone AKTİF)
1. **Soulstone self always pre-set** (30 min CD). Soul Shard her zaman üzerinde.
2. **Pet alive** = priority.
3. **Fear** in open area only.
4. **Healthstone** ready (5 min CD).
5. **Death Coil** emergency heal.
