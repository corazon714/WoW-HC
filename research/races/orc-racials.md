# Orc — Racial Rehberi (Hardcore)

**Versiyon:** Classic Era 1.15.x / Anniversary HC (Classic Fresh)
**Son Güncelleme:** 2026-06-18
**Faction:** Horde
**Mevcut Class'lar:** Warrior, Hunter, Rogue, Shaman, Warlock

## Özet
Orc = HC'nin "anti-stun + burst race"i. **Hardiness** (+%25 stun resistance) HC'de mob stun mekaniklere karşı kalkan. **Blood Fury** AP burst (Warrior, Hunter, Rogue, Shaman Enhancement). Axe Specialization tüm Orc fighters için DPS bonus.

---

## 1. Racial Yetenekler

### 1.1 Blood Fury (Active, 2min CD)
- **+25% (later patches +20%) Attack Power** 15 sn.
- Healing taken **-%50** 25 sn (after blood fury).
- **HC risk**: healing reduction sırasında ölüm riski.
- Burst CD; Mak'gora opener.

### 1.2 Hardiness (Passive)
- **+%25 stun resistance.**
- **HC'de altın**: Stun mob (gnoll, ogre, undead, BRD bosses) resist chance.

### 1.3 Command (Passive)
- **+%5 pet damage.**
- **Class etkisi**: Hunter, Warlock pet damage.

### 1.4 Axe Specialization (Passive)
- **Axe (1H ve 2H) expertise +%1.**
- Warrior, Hunter, Shaman, Rogue axe spec.

## 3. HC-Spesifik Strateji

### 3.1 Hardiness = Stun Survival
- Mob stun mekanikler (gnoll Kick, ogre Stomp, BRD Anvilrage Stun, etc.).
- %25 resist = ~1/4 stun escape chance.
- Combo with PvP trinket (none in HC).

### 3.2 Blood Fury Timing
- Open fight with full HP; **Blood Fury → AP burst → kill fast** before heal reduction matters.
- Avoid Blood Fury low-HP — healing reduction = death.

### 3.3 Axe Specialization
- Warrior leveling weapon choice: Axe (Stoneslayer Uldaman, Anvilrage Officer's Axe BRD).
- Hunter: melee axe (Krol Blade no axe; alternatives lower).
- Shaman: Stoneslayer (Uldaman 2H axe).

### 3.4 Command (Hunter/Warlock)
- +%5 pet dmg → faster solo killing.
- Combo with Beast Mastery (Hunter) + Demonology (Warlock).

## 5. HC-Spesifik Uyarılar

- **Starting zone (Durotar)** orta-tehlike: Skull Rock cave (Burning Blade Felsworn), Tiragarde Keep (Daphne 4-5 ogre spawn).
- **Barrens (10-25)** Horde primary leveling; Centaur Khan elite, Kolkar packs, Razormane Quilboar village.
- **Stonetalon (15-27)** — Charred Vale shared with Alliance.
- **Ashenvale (18-30)** — Astranaar dışı tehlike.
