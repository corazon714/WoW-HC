# Dwarf — Racial Rehberi (Hardcore)

**Versiyon:** Classic Era 1.15.x / Anniversary HC (Classic Fresh)
**Son Güncelleme:** 2026-06-18
**Faction:** Alliance
**Mevcut Class'lar:** Warrior, Paladin, Hunter, Rogue, Priest

## Özet
Dwarf = HC'nin "anti-poison/bleed/disease" race'i. **Stoneform** active racial = bleed/poison/disease anlık temizleme + 10% armor 8 sn. Gun specialization Hunter için BiS, Frost Resistance +10. HC'de en güçlü Warrior/Hunter/Priest race'lerinden biri.

---

## 1. Racial Yetenekler

### 1.1 Stoneform (Active, 3min CD, 8 sec)
- **Bleed, Poison, Disease debuff'ları anında temizler.**
- +10% armor 8 sn.
- **HC'de #1 useful active racial** (Druid Rip bleed, Hunter Serpent Sting, Rogue poison).
- Anti-DoT mob (Spider venom, Worgen rake) için kritik.

### 1.2 Gun Specialization (Passive)
- **Gun expertise +%1** (Hunter ranged, Warrior emergency ranged).
- **Hunter Dwarf BiS race** — gun BiS endgame (Larvae Spewer, Bloodseeker).

### 1.3 Frost Resistance (Passive)
- **+10 Frost Resistance.**
- **HC value**: Winterspring, Frostmane Trolls, Frost mobs.

### 1.4 Find Treasure (Passive Toggle)
- Mini-map'te treasure chest gösterir.
- **HC value**: low. Chest loot zayıf.

### 1.5 (HC notu)
- Dwarf'ın 4 racial'ı var; tüm Alliance race'lerden orta seviyede güçlü.

## 2. Class × Dwarf Önerileri

| Class | Dwarf Sinerji | Tier (HC) |
|---|---|---|
| **Warrior** | Stoneform (bleed/poison purge) | **S** |
| **Paladin** | Stoneform + Frost Resist | **A** |
| **Hunter** | Gun Spec + Stoneform | **S** |
| **Rogue** | Stoneform (own poison wipe? no, target poison; useful) | **B** |
| **Priest** | Stoneform + Fear Ward (Dwarf Priest racial spell!) | **S** |

### 2.1 Dwarf Priest = **Fear Ward!**
- **Dwarf Priest exclusive spell**: Fear Ward (lvl 20 quest).
- **Caster 10 sec immunity to next fear.**
- **HC**: Warlock fear, Stitches fear, dungeon mob fear → Dwarf Priest unique advantage.

## 3. HC-Spesifik Strateji

### 3.1 Stoneform CD Discipline
- Always available; **3 minute CD = available every fight**.
- Use against:
  - Spider Venom (Duskwood, Westfall).
  - Worgen Rake (Duskwood, Silverpine).
  - Naga Poison (BFD, Maraudon).
  - Plagued Hounds (Plaguelands).
  - Scarab burst poison (Silithus, AQ).

### 3.2 Gun Spec for Hunter
- 1.15 BiS guns (community): **Larvae Spewer** (Tier 3 raid), **Striker's Mark** (LBRS), **Hurricane** (UBRS).
- Always use gun > bow as Dwarf Hunter.

### 3.3 Fear Ward (Priest Dwarf)
- **HC altın spell.** Tank pre-pull cast → tank fear-immune 10 sn.
- Stitches Duskwood encounter: Fear Ward → tank holds → grup survival.
