# Tauren — Racial Rehberi (Hardcore)

**Versiyon:** Classic Era 1.15.x / Anniversary HC (Classic Fresh)
**Son Güncelleme:** 2026-06-18
**Faction:** Horde
**Mevcut Class'lar:** Warrior, Hunter, Shaman, Druid

## Özet
Tauren = HC'nin "fat HP + anti-pack" race'i. **War Stomp** (active AoE stun 2 sec) HC'de #1 multi-mob save. **Endurance** (+%5 max HP) sustained survival. Herbalism +15 skill bonus. Druid/Warrior için en güçlü Horde race.

---

## 1. Racial Yetenekler

### 1.1 War Stomp (Active, 2min CD, 0.5 sec cast)
- **2 sec AoE stun** 8 yard radius (max 5 targets).
- **HC altın**: multi-mob pull → War Stomp → bandage / heal / kite / Hearth.
- Druid bear form, Warrior, Shaman, Hunter (out of form) — all can use.

### 1.2 Endurance (Passive)
- **+%5 max HP.**
- Sustained survival; combat'ta her zaman aktif.

### 1.3 Cultivation (Passive)
- **+15 Herbalism skill.**
- HC value: Alchemy/Herbalism profession path.

### 1.4 Nature Resistance (Passive)
- **+10 Nature Resistance.**
- HC value: Druid mob, AQ silithid, plague.

---

## 3. HC-Spesifik Strateji

### 3.1 War Stomp = Anti-Multi-Pull
- Multi-mob aggro → War Stomp → 2 sec stun all → kite / heal / Vanish-equivalent.
- **HC kullanım örnekleri:**
  - Defias multi-pull (Westfall).
  - Centaur Kolkar pack (Barrens).
  - Plagued Hound pack (Plaguelands).
  - Bloodsail Buccaneer multi-pull (STV).
- **0.5 sec cast** = interruptible if hit during cast. **Bear Form War Stomp uninterruptable** (form spell immune).

### 3.2 Endurance Sustained
- +%5 HP = +200-300 HP at lvl 60.
- Sustained edge over Orc Hardiness.

### 3.3 Cultivation (Herbalism)
- Herb skill +15 → Alchemy combo.
- HC: own Free Action Potion craft.

### 3.4 Tauren Druid Special
- Bear/Cat Form War Stomp = uninterruptable stun.
- **HC #1 panic button.**
