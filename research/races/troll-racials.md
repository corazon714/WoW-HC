# Troll — Racial Rehberi (Hardcore)

**Versiyon:** Classic Era 1.15.x / Anniversary HC (Classic Fresh)
**Son Güncelleme:** 2026-06-18
**Faction:** Horde
**Mevcut Class'lar:** Warrior, Hunter, Rogue, Priest, Mage, Shaman

## Özet
Troll = HC'nin "regen + bow spec + beast burst" race'i. **Berserking** = haste burst (sub-30% HP); Hunter için **Bow Specialization** BiS endgame; Regeneration +%10 OoC + Spirit-based. Hunter Troll = en güçlü Horde Hunter.

---

## 1. Racial Yetenekler

### 1.1 Berserking (Active, 3min CD)
- **+10-30% attack/casting speed** 10 sn.
- **Bonus scales with HP**: 100% HP = +10%, 40% HP = +30%, low HP = max +30%.
- **HC dikkat**: low HP berserking = DPS burst ama ölüm riski yüksek.

### 1.2 Regeneration (Passive)
- **+%10 health regen.**
- **+%10 in combat** (unique to Troll, no other race regens in combat naturally).
- HC value: sustained sustain, no other race has this.

### 1.3 Beast Slaying (Passive)
- **+%5 damage to Beasts.**
- HC value: Stranglethorn (gorilla, tiger), Hinterlands (raptor), pet hunter PvP.

### 1.4 Bow Specialization (Passive)
- **Bow expertise +%1.**
- **Hunter Troll BiS race for bow** (Rhok'delar, Ashjre'thul).

### 1.5 Throwing Specialization (Passive)
- **Throwing weapon expertise +%1.**
- Warrior/Rogue ranged throwable.

## 3. HC-Spesifik Strateji

### 3.1 Berserking Timing
- **HC ironic**: Berserking scales with LOWER HP. **Sub-40% HP = +30% haste.**
- **HC öneri**: kullanırken full HP'ye yakın (low HP = ölüm riski + Berserking = paradox).
- Mak'gora: opener at 100% HP → +10% haste burst.

### 3.2 Regeneration Sustain
- **Combat'ta bile +%10 regen.**
- Druid Rejuvenation gibi pasif sustained.
- HC: long fight'ta üst üste eklenir.

### 3.3 Bow Specialization
- Hunter Troll: Rhok'delar epic bow class quest BiS.
- Endgame: Ashjre'thul, Crossbow of Smiting (BWL).

### 3.4 Beast Slaying
- STV (Big Game Hunter quest hattı): +%5 dmg = noticable.
- Pet Hunter PvP: vs other Hunter pets.

## 4. PvP / Mak'gora Notları

- **Berserking** burst opener.
- **Regen** sustain advantage.
- **Beast Slaying** vs Hunter pet.

## 5. HC-Spesifik Uyarılar

- **Starting zone (Durotar)** Orc ile aynı.
- **Barrens (10-25)** Orc/Tauren ile aynı.
- **Stranglethorn (30-45)** Troll race avantajı: Beast Slaying. Ama Bloodsail Buccaneer multi-pull risk.
- **Hinterlands (40-50)** — Vilebranch Troll Village (Jintha'Alor) HC #1 zorluk; Troll race burada lore-friendly ama mob hala düşman.
