# Gnome — Racial Rehberi (Hardcore)

**Versiyon:** Classic Era 1.15.x / Anniversary HC (Classic Fresh)
**Son Güncelleme:** 2026-06-18
**Faction:** Alliance
**Mevcut Class'lar:** Warrior, Rogue, Priest, Mage, Warlock

## Özet
Gnome = HC'nin "caster intelligence" race'i. **+5% Intellect** = +5% mana pool, +%5 spell crit chance scaling. **Escape Artist** = root/snare break (FAP eşdeğer racial). **Engineering Specialization** = profession bonus. Mage/Warlock için en güçlü Alliance race.

---

## 1. Racial Yetenekler

### 1.1 Escape Artist (Active, 1min 45sec CD)
- **Root + snare break** instant.
- **HC altın**: Frost Nova root, Druid Entangling Roots, Hunter Frost Trap freeze.
- 1m45s CD = almost every fight.

### 1.2 Expansive Mind (Passive)
- **+%5 Intellect.**
- **Class etkisi**:
  - **Mage** — +5% mana pool, +%5 spell crit (Int → crit scaling).
  - **Warlock** — same as Mage.
  - **Priest** — Int → mana pool.
  - **Warrior, Rogue** — minimal value.

### 1.3 Arcane Resistance (Passive)
- **+10 Arcane Resistance.**
- **HC value**: vs Mage mob, AQ40 silithid (some Arcane), Naxx caster.

### 1.4 Engineering Specialization (Passive)
- **+%15 Engineering skill.**
- **HC altın**: Engineering profession HC için S tier (parachute, jumper cables, dynamite).
- Gnome Engineer is canonical.

## 3. HC-Spesifik Strateji

### 3.1 Escape Artist = FAP eşdeğer
- Free Action Potion (Alchemy) yerine racial.
- Gold tasarrufu (FAP reagent ~30s).
- Frost Trap (Hunter), Frost Nova (Mage), Entangling Roots (Druid), Hamstring (Warrior) immediate break.

### 3.2 Engineering Specialization
- Gnome ile Engineering öğrenmek daha hızlı.
- Mithril Mechanical Dragonling, Goblin Sapper, Jumper Cables tüm Eng craft access.

### 3.3 Mage Sinerji
- +%5 Int → +%5 mana pool → daha uzun fight.
- HC kite uzun sürer (Frost Mage) → mana avantajı.

### 3.4 Warlock Sinerji
- +%5 Int → daha çok Shadow Bolt cast.
- Engineering Sapper + Lock burst combo.

## 4. PvP / Mak'gora Notları

- **Escape Artist** mid-Mak'gora root break.
- **+%5 Int** Mage 1v1 advantage.

## 5. HC-Spesifik Uyarılar

- **Starting zone (Dun Morogh)** Dwarf ile aynı; çok güvenli.
- **Loch Modan, Wetlands** Dwarf zonleriyle aynı.
- **Northshire / Coldridge Valley early quest** Gnome için ekstra short.

> **HC** Gnomeregan racial home zone (lvl 24-33 dungeon) HC'de skip yaygın. Engineering Gnome questi için sadece "Workshop quest" sub-section yap.
