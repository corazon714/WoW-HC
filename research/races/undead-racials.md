# Undead (Forsaken) — Racial Rehberi (Hardcore)

**Versiyon:** Classic Era 1.15.x / Anniversary HC (Classic Fresh)
**Son Güncelleme:** 2026-06-18
**Faction:** Horde
**Mevcut Class'lar:** Warrior, Rogue, Priest, Mage, Warlock

## Özet
Undead = HC'nin "anti-fear/charm" race'i. **Will of the Forsaken (WotF)** = HC'nin **ikinci canı** (fear/charm/sleep break + immunity 5 sec). Cannibalize = HP refill from humanoid/undead corpses. Underwater Breathing extra time.

---

## 1. Racial Yetenekler

### 1.1 Will of the Forsaken (Active, 2min CD)
- **Fear, Charm, Sleep effects'i anında temizler.**
- 5 sec immunity to those.
- **HC'de #1 emergency racial.**
- Warlock fear, Stitches fear, dungeon mob fear → instant break.
- **HC'de hayat kurtarır.** Fear-into-pack ölümünü engeller.

### 1.2 Cannibalize (Active, 2min CD, 10 sec channel)
- **Humanoid veya Undead corpse'tan HP refill** 7% per 2 sec.
- 10 sec channel = 35% HP recover.
- **HC value**: out-of-combat HP refill; bandage alternative.

### 1.3 Shadow Resistance (Passive)
- **+10 Shadow Resistance.**
- **HC value**: Warlock mob, Scholomance Shadow caster, Naxx Shadow.

### 1.4 Underwater Breathing (Passive)
- **300% longer** underwater breath.
- **HC value**: Stranglethorn naga reef, BFD underwater, Vashj'ir? (no, TBC).
- Drowning prevention.

## 3. HC-Spesifik Strateji

### 3.1 Will of the Forsaken = Anti-Fear God
- 2 minute CD = available every long fight.
- **Use cases:**
  - Warlock fear (Mak'gora).
  - Stitches fear (Duskwood encounter).
  - Strat Live Crimson Inquisitor fear.
  - Scholomance fear.
  - LBRS Beast fear.

### 3.2 Cannibalize Discipline
- Post-combat: kill humanoid mob → walk to corpse → Cannibalize 10 sec channel → 35% HP refill.
- **HC bandage gold tasarrufu.**
- Cannot use during combat.

### 3.3 Underwater Breathing
- Stranglethorn Vile Reef quest: undead can stay 5x longer.
- Drowning prevention (HC death cause).
