# Night Elf — Racial Rehberi (Hardcore)

**Versiyon:** Classic Era 1.15.x / Anniversary HC (Classic Fresh)
**Son Güncelleme:** 2026-06-18
**Faction:** Alliance
**Mevcut Class'lar:** Warrior, Hunter, Rogue, Priest, Druid

## Özet
Night Elf = HC'nin **#1 survival race**'i. **Shadowmeld** = out-of-combat stealth → patrol skip, aggro reset, ölüm önleme. **Wisp** (death speed) HC'de geçersiz (death = death). +%1 dodge passive sustain. HC için en güvenli race seçeneklerinden biri özellikle Druid/Rogue/Hunter için.

---

## 1. Racial Yetenekler

### 1.1 Shadowmeld (Active, instant, no CD restrictions when meeting criteria)
- **Out-of-combat stealth** (combat dışında).
- Move/attack/cast → meld breaks.
- **HC'de #1 utility racial.**
- **Combat aggro dump**: aggro alındığında combat'tan çıkıp Shadowmeld → mob de-aggro 8-15 sn → tekrar safe.
  - Critical: only works if you're not in active combat (out-of-combat olmak için ~6 sn no-damage).
- Patrol skip: mob yaklaşırken Shadowmeld → patrol geçer.

### 1.2 Quickness (Passive)
- **+%1 Dodge** her zaman.
- Sustained survival boost.

### 1.3 Wisp Spirit (Passive)
- Ghost form +50% speed.
- **HC'de değersiz** — ölüm = ölüm, ghost form yok.

### 1.4 Nature Resistance (Passive)
- **+10 Nature Resistance.**
- **HC value**: Druids Wrath, Plagued mobs (sometimes nature), insect poison.

## 3. HC-Spesifik Strateji

### 3.1 Shadowmeld Aggro Dump
- Aggro tehlikesinde:
  1. Stop attacking (out of combat preparation).
  2. Wait until "no combat" status (~6 sec no damage).
  3. Shadowmeld activate.
  4. Mob de-aggro 8-15 sec.
  5. Safe distance walk away.
- **Combat'ın ortasında Shadowmeld çalışmaz** (must be out of combat).
- **Workaround:** Druid → Travel Form → run → Shadowmeld → safe.

### 3.2 Patrol Skip
- Stealth mobs detect değil Shadowmelded'i.
- Mob aggro radius içinde sessizce yaklaş → Shadowmeld → mob geçer → re-engage.

### 3.3 Druid Shadowmeld Synergy
- Druid combat form → drop form → Shadowmeld → re-form.
- Aggro reset + form switch = combat reset.
- **HC Druid signature move.**

### 3.4 NE Priest Starshards
- Mage Polymorph eşdeğer pulling kit (NE Priest exclusive).
- DoT damage outside spell budget; **HC sustain için yeterli**.

### 3.5 Nature Resistance
- Druid/Hunter pet's nature damage azalır.
- Plaguelands plagued mob (occasional nature) cuts.
